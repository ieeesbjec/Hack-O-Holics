About Hack-O-Holics 3.0

(A National Level Hackathon Organised by IEEE Student Branch Jeppiaar Engineering College in Hybrid Mode)

Hack-O-Holics is conducted to intent energize young ingenious minds to contribute irrespective of their ages and to have a new experience. It is not just a part of IEEE Day, but also with a purpose to inspire our nation’s youth to think out of the box, widen ideas by thinking, and reach the crown of fortune in all peculiar domains. The event’s main objective is to bring up innovative ideas and solutions for various statements and real-time crises prevailing in our society.where various teams will be participating and go ahead establishing their ideas in projects on real-time difficulties. We're aiming to deliver great insights, networking, and accomplish personalities from all over the world. It has been very successful over the years that the first season of H-O-H received the 2021 Darrel Chong Student Activity Award Silver. The event will be spectacular in fixing to transfer the latest technology advances and making a step forward from the comfort zone to challenge and compete with the society of extraordinary thinking. Surely this event will be a grand success with massive ideas and thoughts by improving the betterment of society.

Team Formation

A team can be consist of maximum four members. They should have their own team name and must submit the abstract softcopy before the Event.

Fee details

Prize for winners

Why Should you participate?

The main idea behind HOH is to identify a problem and work together to create technologies that give solutions to that problem. It gives chances to students to explore their idea for problems that solve current affairs and are good for future development. This is synonymous with fun. Yes, you will be challenged and you will be stressed at some point but you will enjoy every minute of it. Being surrounded by very interesting people, and having your brain stimulated with new technologies is a great combination for having fun. There are a few more aims for your participation in this Hack O Holics, which include:
  1. Best platform to share your ideas.
  2. You will learn team-building skills.
  3. Can apply in Resumes.
  4. Cash prizes and Free stuff.
  5. Can be engaged in fun-filled activities, webinars, etc.
